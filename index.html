Bulid Superpowers In Your Skills

The Hack Club - North Developer Community is a computer programming club at Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Punjab and is a chapter of the International Hack Club organization. We meet weekly on Saturday.

Our Vision

The vision behind the initiation of a Hack Club is to build an environment where every student and developer can learn, build, and share their projects, ideas, and vision.

A group of students gather to start coding. Many are beginners.
Everyone starts hacking.
At the first few meetings, members follow some tutorials as a runway before taking off with their own projects.

Members work on self-directed coding projects and modules, learning by making.
Members leave every meeting with a project
including their first day. Create your first website & game at Hack Clubs.

At the end, everyone demos their projects for the club.
Demos bring the club together.
As the leader, you’re cultivating a community of makers. Demos highlight new work & momentum.
